# How Renewals Work

Synthetic internal knowledge note.

1. CRM is the starting point for renewal dates.
2. Account managers review customers due in the next 45 days.
3. Finance checks aged debt and disputes before renewal terms are sent.
4. Sales checks whether any special pricing was agreed by email.
5. Operations checks open service issues.
6. Renewal letter is sent only when the account manager confirms the final terms.

## Known Messy Parts

- CRM and the renewal spreadsheet do not always match.
- Discounts are sometimes agreed in email and not copied to CRM.
- Finance may block renewal where an invoice dispute is unresolved.
- Operations may need to confirm service issues before renewal.
