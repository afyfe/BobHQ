What is AskBob?

AskBob is an early product idea for helping SMEs ask practical questions about their own operations, documents, decisions, and working context.

Operational

Focused on how work actually happens across people, files, systems, and habits.

Grounded

Designed around source-aware answers, uncertainty, and review rather than guesswork.

Practical

Intended for everyday business questions before larger platform work is justified.
