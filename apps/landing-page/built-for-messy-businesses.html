Operational reality

Built for messy businesses

Most businesses do not run from a perfect system of record. Work is spread across inboxes, spreadsheets, chat, documents, calls, and memory.

  • Important context often lives outside the official process.
  • Different teams use different names for the same customer, supplier, or project.
  • Decisions are made with partial information and cleaned up later.
