# Discovery Mode

## Purpose

Discovery Mode allows Bob to safely inspect and understand a customer's operational landscape before full onboarding.

## Goals

- Reduce onboarding fear
- Reveal operational fragmentation
- Surface hidden systems and documents
- Demonstrate value quickly
- Build trust

## Discovery Examples

Bob may identify:

- SharePoint sites
- File shares
- Sage exports
- CRM systems
- Operational spreadsheets
- Shared inboxes
- Legacy document stores
- Duplicate customer datasets

## Discovery States

### Discovered
Bob found the source.

### Inferred
Bob believes the source is operationally important.

### Confirmed
A human confirmed the source relevance.

### Rejected
The source should be ignored.

## Trust Principles

- Discovery is read-only
- Customer controls activation
- Bob explains findings
- Bob shows provenance
- Bob never pretends certainty

## Holy Shit Moment

"We found 14 operational spreadsheets and 3 disconnected customer data sources."
