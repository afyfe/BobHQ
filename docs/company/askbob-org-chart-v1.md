# Overview

This document captures the very early AskBob organisational structure as the project evolves from concept into a real operational business.

The structure reflects the core AskBob philosophy:

> Build useful, grounded operational technology with real human understanding behind it.

This is not intended to be a rigid corporate hierarchy.

It is a lightweight founder-era structure defining the natural strengths and responsibilities forming around the AskBob project.

---

# Founding Philosophy

AskBob is being built around:

- Real-world SME operational experience
- Practical systems thinking
- Finance and process understanding
- Human communication
- Trust and operational visibility
- Family culture and grounded values

Core principle:

> NAIB — Not Another AI Bollocks.
