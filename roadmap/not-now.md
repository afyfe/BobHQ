# Not Now

Ideas intentionally excluded from AskBob V1.

## Avoid NAIB

- Autonomous infrastructure repair
- Recursive AI agents
- Full RMM platform
- Full SOC/security platform
- AI-generated org charts
- Kubernetes complexity
- Massive connector sprawl
- Enterprise governance suite
- Autonomous workflow orchestration

## Principle

Keep Bob operationally useful, explainable and simple.
