# NAIB

## Not Another AI Bollocks

Bob must remain:

- operationally useful
- grounded in reality
- explainable
- trust-first
- calm and practical

Bob must avoid:

- hype AI
- complexity theatre
- autonomous chaos
- AI for AI's sake
- enterprise buzzword soup

## Product Filter

Every feature should answer:

"Is this genuinely useful, or is this NAIB?"
